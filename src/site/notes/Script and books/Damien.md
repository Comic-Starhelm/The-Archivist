Case_01
One of the few memories Damien remembers is getting his doctorate— throwing his graduation cap into the air, hugging his loved ~~dead, and decaying, ripped of life~~ one and breathing a sigh of relief.

The next thing he remembers is a cold interrogation room, a table in the middle, two seats in front of it and him on the other side. He remembers the static in his ears, the consequences of two hours of sleep, and an ache stabbing into his head.
His work place was dodgy, he knew that— but he didn't expect to be involved in an organ harvesting ring.
Sure, he liked research— a bit too much.. the mind is such an interesting aspect of a mortal being. Where did consciousness come from? Are we just vibrations thrown into physical forms asked to survive in a world that's drowning in debt.
Don't forget about the body— he dreamt for the day the body wouldn't have to live without the most important part of a human being.

*The heart.*

He doesn't remember the majority of that interrogation. But, he remembers how he was there for eight grueling hours, no food in his system, nor any water. Severely dehydrated and hungry, these detectives can't do this to him according to the law— but again.. he did.. something to deserve it.

Then the door banged open, the detectives were long gone to be honest, he didn't know the time but he was sure it was after midnight at least. He saw three men, two entered— one stood outside away from his vision. He remembers one sitting down and pushing a huge document towards him and one handing him water and a sweet bun.

“ Do you want out of here? We need people like you. ”


He could only agree, he didn't have any other option.

Case_03
H.E.L.P. was— not what he expected. At least not this.. warm. He expected a brutalist interior, white glowing lights that pierced into your eyes and a cold marble floor. Sure, there are its locations which share that similarity, like its research sector. He also didn't expect so many.. anomalies, critters of different kinds, species different from an average human. It made him feel jealous— he was, but a human!

Time went by, he quickly rose the ranks and gained respect, often being called the teacher's pet of the administration. He didn't confront it, why bother to communicate with idiots? Some rumours also spoke of him being the director due to their anonymity.. while that was totally wrong, it was an entertaining rumour.
