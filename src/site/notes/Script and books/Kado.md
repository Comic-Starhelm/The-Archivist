More info:
Kado has a robotic arm that can be turned into a weapon of his choice when it is needed, though it does take a lot of power. The arm is detachable but it would cause Kado severe pain as if his actual arm was being cut off again

Kado is often seen as "rude" or "mean" due to his natural trust issues, especially after the "incident" with his family. He had two younger siblings and was recruited by the director after the director saw his abilities in a mission 

Kado also has a habit of drinking when hes bored, stressed or anxious, he uses this as a way to cope with his daily life especially when he is working
