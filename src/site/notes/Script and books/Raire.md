Log_01
Rite, or as they call themselves now, Raire, grew up on the peaceful coral reefs of south-east ocean. they lived with their mother and two siblings on a house with these like high 'legs' idk what that is called. even heavier rains barely ever showed up, letting the community, build around the ocean and the reef, prosper without bigger challanges - kids went to school, everybody had enough food and a shelter to stay at, you could always ask one of the older ppl for help if you needed any. but, when Rite was 13, a massive storm came. it absolutely destroyed everything they ever knew and killed all of the people they ever loved. Rite didn't know how they survived, all they remembered after was a girl that took them out of the collapsed home and gave them the raft they survived on.
They woke up, on a raft, with resources enough for surviving a month alone
they drifted until they got to the land - but it wasnt the warm, sunny beaches of south-east reef, but rather the pirate ship cementary, swept with the freezing winds of Fjerda. They chose to live on the biggest ship, which was the least destroyed. there, Rite took the name of the constellation that shined over their first home - Raire, or "The  Ray and the Crab". they spent there a few years, studying the arcanes and training with a long blade and the bow.
