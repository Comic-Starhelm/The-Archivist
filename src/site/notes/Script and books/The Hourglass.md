Case_01
What a cold place. 

Clocks ticking in an endless white labyrinth.
It’s like they are watching my every step.

It’s the same feeling as back then when I was being treated for my illness. 
My body was fragile as glass. one sneeze can shatter it. The director saved me and saw my potential and offered work under him.

This place is very distorted. I don’t know if there’s an end to it.
Or maybe I never moved no matter how many steps I took. It’s a labyrinth after all 

Doesn’t matter. I’m use to it now.
They can judge me all they want because in the end. This is my own domain and I have a something important to do.

The H.E.L.P. Council are in danger in the near future. An intruder will expose all of our identities to the world. I need to pick a path where everyone doesn’t always need think about danger. 

I finally reached a glass platform with moving clock hands underneath.
At the center stand sky (my companion) and an hourglass shaped from a pocket watch split in the middle with the hands are still functioning normally. It was covered in a crystal bubble.

“So this is where you’ve been resting. Thank you for your help, sky.”

I gave him a pat on the head as a reward. Maybe I’ll give him some berries too.

Sky chirp happily and then he glows and bursts into particals. It broke the crystal bubble in the process and replaced with numbers circling and form a ring around it.

I reach my hand out and took control over the artifact with the power sky lend me.

H.E.L.P became my home. Every meeting is chaotic. I only watched for a far and made myself small because I can’t bare to interrupt their movements. 
If I have to trade my life to save this organization. I’ll do it without a second thought.

The artifact began to shook violently and created golden strings of possibilities. The taste of iron filled my senses. It hurts. the fluctuations of the labyrinth changing as I pick the path of the future i want.

Then it stop just for a brief moment before everything turns black.
