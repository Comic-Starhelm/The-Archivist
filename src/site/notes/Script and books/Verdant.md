## INTERVIEW ||###||, ‘VERDANT’

“Welcome, and thank you for taking this interview!”

“Of course.”

*The video of two people in an interview room, one person, holding a clipboard, sits with good posture. The person sitting across from them is sitting cross-legged, despite being in a chair.*

“So, before we get to the real questions, what is your name?”

“I’m Verdant Trees! Don’t worry about checking any files, I’m not there.”

*The person sitting cross-legged, ‘Verdant’, shrugs.*

V: “Kinda rude they didn’t include me…”

*The interviewer raises an eyebrow.*

I: “An odd name. Is there any special meaning or background behind it?”

V: “Yip. But that’s not anything that’d be… uh… words… coherent? To you?”

I: “Try me.”

V: “Eh, sure, why not. Every diaphantomae, after forming, isn’t given, but knows their name. The connection of the name to their unique concept is usually clear, but more abstract elements can prompt the formation of a vaguer name. Mine’s quite clear, no?”

I: “Diaphantom…?”

*Verdant shrugs again.*
