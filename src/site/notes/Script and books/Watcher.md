Case_01
Name: [REDACTED]
Codename: Watcher
Department: Science
Division: Enchantment
Age: Unknown
Recruited by The Archivist
Appearance: Always wearing a cloak and mask, though type and color vary. They are most commonly seen wearing a gray cloak accented with green, along with a simple black mask, also accented with green.

*Upon examining the text below, you notice it seems to have been written over several months*

Notes:
Watcher is unique, due to the fact their “rebirth cycle” makes them functionally immortal. They have not stated if there are any limits to this, though weather they don’t know or simply don’t want to share is unknown.

They work in the Scientific Department, more specifically the Division of Enchanting. They are one of the best in said Division, and have made some of H.E.L.P.’s strongest tools and weapons.

When startled they let out a ‘Honk’ which, despite what its name suggests, does not sounds like a honk. Sometimes it sounds different, but they have assured us it is nothing to worry about.

Despite their hands being completely functional, they insist on holding things with their magic. When asked why, they have simply stated, “Magic is cooler.”

They are unskilled in combat, likely due to the relatively peaceful nature of their home, and usually leave fighting to other H.E.L.P. members. Let it be known they are not defenseless, as some unfortunate new members have discovered.  (Though honestly, they had it coming.)

Case_02
Has stated on several occasions that their home would be a ‘great vacation spot’ to other H.E.L.P members. So far, no one has taken them up on their offer of a tour. On that note, they request a break monthly to return there. There have been some deviations to this, but they were likely due to events of some kind.

Case_03
Never shine a red spotlight on or near them. It triggers their fight or flight, and usually results in them attempting to get out of said spotlight as quickly as they can.
